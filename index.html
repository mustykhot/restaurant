About

Imperial donut is an independently owned and family run business that specilizes in the creation of artisan donuts. Established in early 2004, we have been blown away by the unexpected success and popularity of our carefully crafted donuts.our most popular donuts include the honey glazed donut, the jam-filled donut and our ice-glazed donut.

Currently, we make 117 different types of donuts on a daily-basis and have two stores in the toronto area, one store in montreal and one store in chicago. Definitely pop by one of our locations to try at least one of our donuts but if you can't make it don't worry! we've recently introduced both a catering service for those special locations such as weddings & birthdays and have also recently introduced a delivery service for our surprise donut collection box which contains 6 different donut flavours.
